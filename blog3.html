Negative Impact of Technology in the Watch Industry
20 June 2023 | James Smith

Technology has brought many positive changes to the watch industry, but there are also some negative impacts. One major downside is the rise of smartwatches. Some people feel that traditional watches are losing their charm because of smartwatches. Quick updates in technology can make certain models feel outdated too fast, which can frustrate buyers.

The rise of digital technology in watches has made them more vulnerable to cybersecurity risks. Smartwatches that store personal data, like health information and payment details, could be hacked, leading to privacy concerns for users.
